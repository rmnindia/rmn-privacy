Privacy Policy for RMN Classified

Effective Date: May 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how RMN Classified ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects the information provided by users of our mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect basic user information such as:
    • Full Name
    • Email Address
  • Device Information: We may collect non-personal information such as device type, OS version, and app version for analytics or troubleshooting.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your precise location to display nearby ads in the WebView. Location access is optional and can be denied at any time via device settings.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect the data to:

  • Enable users to post and view classified ads
  • Display nearby location-based ads
  • Support login and user management
  • Maintain and improve app performance

3. Data Retention

All data uploaded to RMN Classified is retained for a maximum of 20 days. After that, it is automatically deleted from our servers. No backups of this data are kept beyond this period.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may be processed by Firebase (Google) for authentication and storage purposes in accordance with their policies.

5. Security

All user data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. We take reasonable steps to protect user information from unauthorized access.
